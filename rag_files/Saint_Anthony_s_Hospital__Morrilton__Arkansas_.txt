Saint Anthony's Hospital (Morrilton, Arkansas)

Saint Anthony's Hospital is a historic hospital building at 202 East Green Street in Morrilton, Arkansas.  Built in 1935 to a design by A. N. McAninch, it is an Art Deco building, finished in brick and stone.  It served as the local hospital until 1970, and is now a senior living facility.  It is Morrilton's best example of Art Deco architecture.  It has two splayed wings, with a central projecting entry pavilion.[2]

The building was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1986.[1]
