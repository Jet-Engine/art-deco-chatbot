Landrum's Hamburger System No. 1



The Landrum's Hamburger System No. 1, at 1300 S. Virginia St. in Reno, Nevada, USA, was built in 1947. It was a Moderne style building that has also been known as Chili Cheez Cafe. It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1998.[1][2] The building has since been occupied by Beefy's Burgers.[3] The building encloses 400 square feet (37Â m2) of floor space.[3]
