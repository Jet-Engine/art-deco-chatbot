Stuart Building (Lincoln, Nebraska)



The Stuart Building is a historic 10-story building in Lincoln, Nebraska. It was built by the Olson Construction Company in 1927 for the Stuart Investment Company, founded in 1880.[2] It was designed in the Art Deco and Gothic Revival styles by Ellery L. Davis of Davis and Wilson.[2] It has been listed on the National Register of Historic Places since December 23, 2003.[1]
