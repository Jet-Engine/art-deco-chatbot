Union Storage & Transfer Cold Storage Warehouse and Armour Creamery Building



The Union Storage & Transfer Cold Storage Warehouse and Armour Creamery Building in Fargo, North Dakota, United States, was built in 1930.  It includes work by Fargo architect William F. Kurke.[1][2]
