Dominguez–Wilshire Building

The Dominguez–Wilshire Building is located at 5410 Wilshire Boulevard in the Miracle Mile district of Los Angeles, an Art Deco landmark. The architects were Morgan, Walls & Clements. The building was renovated in 2000.[1]

The property was named after its developers, the Dominguez family, the heirs to the first land grant given in California by King Carlos III of Spain.
