Kearny School



The Kearny School, at 800 S. 3rd St. in Raton, New Mexico, was built in 1936.  It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1996.[1]

It was a Public Works Administration project.

Architecture: Art Deco
Historic function: Education
Historic subfunction: Library
