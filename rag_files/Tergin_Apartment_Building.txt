Tergin Apartment Building

Tergin Apartment Building, also known as Tergin Apartments , is a historic apartment building located at Jefferson City, Cole County, Missouri. It was built in 1938–1939, and is a two-story brick walkup apartment building with a full basement.  It measures 50 feet wide by 35 feet deep and features Art Deco and Streamline Moderne design elements.[2]: 5 

It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1999.[1] It is located in the Missouri State Capitol Historic District.
