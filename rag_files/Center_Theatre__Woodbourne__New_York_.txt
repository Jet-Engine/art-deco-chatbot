Center Theatre (Woodbourne, New York)

Center Theatre, also known as the Woodbourne Theater, is a historic theatre located at Woodbourne in Sullivan County, New York.  It was built in 1938 and is a three bays wide, two stories tall Art Deco structure.  It is three time longer than it is wide and has a large auditorium behind the entrance pavilion.  The entrance pavilion consists of the facade, foyer, and lobby.[2]

It was added to the National Register of Historic Places in 2001.[1]
