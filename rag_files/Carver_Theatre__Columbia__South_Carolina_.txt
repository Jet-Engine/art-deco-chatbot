Carver Theatre (Columbia, South Carolina)

Carver Theatre is a historic African American movie theater in Columbia, South Carolina. It was built in 1941, and is a two-story, rectangular, brick commercial building.  It has a flat roof and a vertical marquee.  It operated as a movie theater until 1971.[2][3]

It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 2003.[1]
