Aurora County Courthouse



The Aurora County Courthouse, located in Plankinton, South Dakota is a building in the Art Moderne and Art Deco styles.

It was built in 1940 and added to the National Register of Historic Places in 1993.  It was one of four county courthouses in South Dakota designed by architects Kings & Dixon.[2]

As of 2013, the building served as the county courthouse for Aurora County.[3]
