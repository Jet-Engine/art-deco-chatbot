Fowler Theatre

Fowler Theatre is a historic theater located at Fowler, Benton County, Indiana. It was built in 1940, and is a one-storey, Art Deco style movie theater. It is a red brick building on a concrete foundation and features the original marquee.  The interior has Art Deco and Art Moderne decorative elements.[2]: 5 

It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 2004.[1]
