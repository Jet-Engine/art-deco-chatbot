Saluda Theatre

Saluda Theatre is a historic movie theater located at Saluda, Saluda County, South Carolina. It was built in 1936, and is a two-story, stuccoed masonry building in the Art Deco style. The theater closed in 1981.[2][3] It was restored after 1987, and is home to the Saluda Players.[4]

It was added to the National Register of Historic Places in 1993.[1]
