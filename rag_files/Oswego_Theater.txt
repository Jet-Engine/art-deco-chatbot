Oswego Theater

Oswego Theater, now known as Oswego 7 Cinemas, is a historic movie theater located at Oswego in Oswego County, New York.  It was designed in 1940 in the Art Deco style and opened in 1941.  The front features bands of yellow, red, and dark red brick that create broad horizontal and perpendicular belts.
