Plaza Hotel (Trenton, Missouri)

Plaza Hotel is a historic hotel building located at Trenton, Grundy County, Missouri.  It was built in 1929–1930, and is a five-story, Art Deco style reinforced concrete building. The building measures approximately 10,912 square feet (1,013.8 m2) It has a pre-cast, concrete block exterior, concrete roof and floors.[2]: 5 

It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 2001.[1]
