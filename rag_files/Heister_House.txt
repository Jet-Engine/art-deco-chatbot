Heister House

Heister House is a Moderne (Art Deco) house built in 1943 in Salida, Colorado.  The building  was listed on the National Register of Historic Places on October 8, 2008.[2][1]

It is the 15th property listed as a featured property of the week in a program of the National Park Service that began in July, 2008.[1][3]
