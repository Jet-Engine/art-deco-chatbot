Ruleton School



The Ruleton School, in Sherman County, Kansas near Goodland, is a historic school that was built in 1928.  It includes Modern Movement architecture and is listed on the National Register of Historic Places.[1]

It was deemed historically significant for its usage as a school and as a community center in its community of Ruleton.  It was built after a bond vote to raise $22,500 was passed by vote of 44 to 26, on March 17, 1928.[2]: 7
