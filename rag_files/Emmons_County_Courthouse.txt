Emmons County Courthouse



Emmons County Courthouse in Linton, North Dakota was built in 1934.  It is notable for being the first Works Progress Administration project granted in the state. Seven courthouses in the state were completed as part of this program.  The Emmons County Courthouse is also one of eight Art Deco courthouses in North Dakota.  The Courthouse was entered into the National Register of Historic Places on 14 November 1985.[2]
