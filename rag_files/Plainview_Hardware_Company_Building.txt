Plainview Hardware Company Building



The Plainview Hardware Company Building, at 210 S. Main St. in Perryton, Texas, was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1990.[1]

It is a two-story masonry Art Deco-style building, with a dressed limestone veneer on its front and common bond brick on its sides and rear.   It is 25 by 140 feet (7.6 m × 42.7 m) in plan.  The Art Deco style is expressed by carved limestone
with bas relief detailing creating angular geometric forms, including some squares, and floral motifs.[2]
