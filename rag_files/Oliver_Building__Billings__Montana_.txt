Oliver Building (Billings, Montana)



The Oliver Building is a historic building in Billings, Montana. It was built in 1910 as a warehouse for Oliver Chilled Plow Works, a manufacturer of tractors and plows.[2] It was remodelled by architect Chandler C. Cohagen in 1930.[2] It has been listed on the National Register of Historic Places since December 19, 2008.[1]
