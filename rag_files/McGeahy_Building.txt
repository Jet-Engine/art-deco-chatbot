McGeahy Building

McGeahy Building is a historic commercial building located at Biltmore Village, Asheville, Buncombe County, North Carolina.  It was built in 1927, and is a two-story, brick building with a raised parapet.[2]

It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1979.[1]
