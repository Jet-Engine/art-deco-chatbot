Earl H. Ellis VFW Post No. 1362



The Earl H. Ellis VFW Post #1362, located at 701 E. 1st St. in Pratt, Kansas, was built in 1939.  It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 2006.[1]

It has been said to constitute "a good example of a late Art Deco structure featuring native stone construction."[2]

It was deemed "significant architecturally and socially as a reflection of the history of the community of Pratt."[2]

The listing included, in addition to the building, two contributing objects.[1]
