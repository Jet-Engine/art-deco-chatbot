Fremont Municipal Auditorium



The Fremont Municipal Auditorium is a historic building in Fremont, Nebraska. It was built in 1937 with funding from the Public Works Administration, and designed in the Art Deco architectural style.[2] It has been listed on the National Register of Historic Places since July 11, 2002.[1]
