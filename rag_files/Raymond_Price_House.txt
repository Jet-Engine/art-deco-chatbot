Raymond Price House

Raymond Price House is a historic home located at Columbia, South Carolina. It was built in 1952, and is a two-story, L-shaped, steel-framed, masonry dwelling in the Streamline Moderne / International style.  It has a flat roof and front and rear balconies. Also on the property is a one-story structure that is now an office.[2][3]

It was added to the National Register of Historic Places in 2007.[1]
