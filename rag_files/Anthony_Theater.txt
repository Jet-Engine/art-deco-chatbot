Anthony Theater



The  Anthony Theater, located at 220 W. Main St. in Anthony, Kansas, is an Art Deco-style theater built in 1936.  It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1991.[1]

It was designed by architect S.S. Voigt.  It is a two-story building with white concrete, buff brick, and copper brown tiles.  It is 40 by 140 feet (12 m × 43 m) in plan.[2]
