US General Accounting Office Building

The U.S. Government Accounting Office Building is an historic government office building, the headquarters of the Government Accountability Office. It is located at 441 G Street NW in Washington, D.C., adjacent to the National Building Museum.[2] The building also serves as headquarters for U.S. Army Corps of Engineers.

It was dedicated on September 11, 1951, by President Harry S. Truman.[3][4]
