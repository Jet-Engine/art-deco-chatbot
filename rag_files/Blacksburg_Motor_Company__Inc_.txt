Blacksburg Motor Company, Inc.

Blacksburg Motor Company, Inc., also known as Doc Roberts Tire Co. and Heavener Chevrolet, is a historic commercial building located at Blacksburg, Montgomery County, Virginia.  It was built in 1924 and expanded about 1933, and is a two-story brick and poured concrete building with only a single story visible from the front facade.  It features Art Deco and Moderne design details including painted concrete details, rounded corners, and bands of windows with metal frames on the fa√ßade and tin ceilings and terrazzo floors on the interior. It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 2008.[1]
