Lane Theater

The Lane Theater in Williamsburg, Kentucky, located at 508 Main St., is an Art Deco-style building which was built in 1948.  It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 2004.[1]

The first-floor level of the facade "is covered in yellow and brown horizontal striped panels of enameled metal, accented with chrome"; the facade further above "is faced in vertical yellow and brown striped panels of enameled metal surrounding second floor glass block windows."[2]
