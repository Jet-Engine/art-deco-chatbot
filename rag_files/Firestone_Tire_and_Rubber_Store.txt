Firestone Tire and Rubber Store

Firestone Tire and Rubber Store is a historic commercial building located in downtown Evansville, Indiana. It was built in 1930, and is a one-story, Art Deco style building.  The building was originally built to house a Firestone Tire and Rubber Company outlet.[2]: Part 1 

It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1984.[1]
