John & Florence McKeage House

John & Florence McKeage House is a national historic site located at 209 Park Street South, St. Petersburg, Florida in Pinellas County. It is an Art Moderne style house constructed in 1938.[2]

It was added to the National Register of Historic Places on April 9, 2013.[3]
